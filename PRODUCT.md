# Product

## Register

product

## Users
People who want to share clean, concise URLs on social media, messaging apps, or presentations. They value speed, privacy, and simplicity. They are tired of cluttered, ad-heavy, and AI-slop styled link shorteners.

## Product Purpose
A fast, elegant, and completely free URL shortener. Success is defined by an instant, friction-free URL shortening experience and sub-100ms server-side redirections.

## Brand Personality
- Minimal
- Precise
- Trustworthy
Voice is quiet but confident. No unnecessary marketing buzzwords, no screen-shouting hero sections.

## Anti-references
- Ad-choked shortener pages with countdown timers.
- Over-designed "AI-built" SaaS clones with giant gradient text headers, floating glowing blobs, and excessive glassmorphism boxes.
- Standard blue/white corporate bootstrap forms.

## Design Principles
1. **Focus on the Input**: The primary action (pasting a link) should be front and center, keyboard-accessible immediately on load.
2. **Invisible Utility**: Redirections should be as direct and lightweight as possible, leaving no visual footprint.
3. **Typographic Grace**: Use premium whitespace, elegant sans-serif/mono fonts, and high contrast instead of borders, cards, and patterns to establish hierarchy.
4. **Honest Copy**: Clearly state what is happening, with direct feedback (e.g. instant copy button) and no marketing fluff.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Full keyboard navigability (focus rings, tab indexes).
- High contrast values conforming to WCAG AA standards (contrast ratio >= 4.5:1 for body and controls).
- Reduced motion support (disabling transitions and micro-animations if requested by system settings).
